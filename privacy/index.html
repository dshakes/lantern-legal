SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This personal messaging assistant sends conversational text messages on behalf of Shekhar Mudarapu ("we", "us") to personal contacts who have initiated contact with him. This policy explains how mobile information is handled.

Information we use

We use your mobile phone number and the content of messages you send solely to read your message and generate and deliver a reply. We do not collect data beyond what is needed to hold the conversation.

How we share information — mobile numbers are not shared

We do not share mobile information (your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in/consent) with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information is sold, rented, or shared for marketing. The only sharing is with our messaging provider (Twilio) and mobile carriers to transmit a message, and an AI model provider to generate a reply. We may disclose information if required by law.

Message frequency, rates, and opt-out

Message frequency varies and depends on the conversation; messages are sent only in reply to messages you send. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP; after opting out you will receive one confirmation and no further messages. Reply HELP for help.

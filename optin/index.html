How recipients opt in to messages

Consent flow for Shekhar Mudarapu's personal messaging assistant (A2P 10DLC / conversational).

The full consent experience, start to finish

  1. The recipient already has the sender's number. Recipients are the sender's existing personal contacts — friends, family, and personal/professional acquaintances.
  2. The recipient initiates the conversation by sending a text message to the sender. This inbound message is the opt-in: by texting the sender first, the recipient consents to receive a reply.
  3. The sender's personal assistant replies on the sender's behalf, only within that recipient-initiated conversation.
  4. No message is ever sent to anyone who has not first texted the sender. There is no marketing, advertising, promotional, or bulk messaging.
  5. The recipient can opt out any time by replying STOP, and reply HELP for assistance.
